We collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and delivery address when you place an order or contact us. We also collect browsing data to improve user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to process your orders, communicate with you about products and promotions, improve our services, and send you updates about your orders.
